Funding principle

The work should serve comprehension first.

CommonGround is aimed at healthier interaction between people, websites, institutions, and intelligent systems. Support helps keep the center of gravity on consent, dignity, and understanding. Donations support open research, prototypes, documentation, privacy architecture, and public-interest tools for comprehension-centered web experiences.

Research

Model the relationship between lived context, purpose, comprehension, and application.

Prototypes

Build working examples of adaptive parsing, protected context, and website integration.

Documentation

Publish clear technical notes, implementation guides, and public-facing explainers.

Privacy

Explore Zero Knowledge Proof (ZKP) patterns that let sensitive context help without becoming exposed data.
